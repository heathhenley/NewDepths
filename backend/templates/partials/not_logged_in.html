What is this?

This is a simple app to send you email notifications when new MBES, CSB, or NOS surveys (w/ digital data) data become available at NOAA's endpoints. Select up to 5 bounding boxes and get notified when new data becomes available in those areas everyday.

NEW! You can also request the actual MBES or CSB from NOAA's point store API for your bounding boxes from your account page, and the data will be sent to your email (defaults to a csv).

Please reach out with any questions, issues, or suggestions!
