What is this?

Receive email notifications when new MBES, CSB, or NOS surveys (w/ digital data) data become available at NOAA's endpoints. You can also request the actual MBES or CSB from NOAA's point store API for your bounding boxes from your account page.

How does it work?

  1. Login or Register
  2. Create a bounding box
  3. Get daily email summary (only when there's new data)
  4. Optionally request data in the box from NOAA
